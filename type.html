Take Part as a Speaker

There are two ways to join the AIMs seminar as a speaker:

Case Track (without paper)

Presentations of a project or exhibition.

Formats

Large Talk: completed or mature projects ⏱ 20 min + 5 min Q&A

Short Talk: emerging ideas or early-stage concepts ⏱ 10 min + 2 min Q&A

Submit title and abstract by email.

Deadline: 05 May – Belgrade 10 June – St. Petersburg

Scientific Track (with paper)

Presentations on topics aligned with the Springer CCIS book.

Formats

Full Research: completed or advanced studies ⏱ 20 min + 5 min Q&A

Early Research: pilot or in-progress studies ⏱ 10 min + 2 min Q&A

Submit via CMT (this track pipeline).

Deadline: 30 Mar

The seminar is held offline. Online participation may be considered individually.
